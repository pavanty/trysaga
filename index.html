About Saga Kapital Group Inc

A full-service contractor for field and operations teams

Saga Kapital Group Inc is a trusted last-mile delivery service provider operating under FedEx Ground, committed to delivering packages safely, efficiently, and on time. We focus on reliability, customer satisfaction, and operational excellence at the final stage of the logistics chain.

Our organization is supported by professional route personnel and operations teams who manage dispatch, compliance, fleet maintenance, and performance nationwide. We hold ourselves to high standards of accountability so every delivery reflects the quality expected of the FedEx network.

Our Team

The people behind every successful delivery

At Saga Kapital Group Inc, our strength comes from the people behind every successful delivery. Our team is made up of dedicated delivery professionals, dispatchers, and operations staff who work together to ensure every package is delivered safely, efficiently, and on time.

As a delivery service provider for FedEx, we take pride in upholding high standards of professionalism, reliability, and customer service. Every team member plays a vital role in representing the FedEx brand at the final point of delivery.

We foster a culture of teamwork, accountability, and continuous improvement, empowering our people to perform at their best while growing their careers in the logistics industry.
